Our Story

Rooted in the Soil of Punjab

Salaria Naturals was born from a single, unwavering belief — that the most nourishing foods come straight from the earth, untouched by chemicals and uncompromised by shortcuts.

We work directly with farming families who have worked this land for generations. Every bottle carries the warmth of Punjab's sun and the hard-earned wisdom of people who understand that real food takes patience.

We use the ancient Kachi Ghani cold-press method — slow, deliberate, preserving every nutrient, every aroma, every reason why this oil has been a staple in Indian kitchens for millennia.

How It's Made

Seed to Bottle — Nothing Hidden

01
Field Harvest

Partner farmers harvest black mustard seeds at peak ripeness — hand-selected for purity and oil content.

02
Clean & Sort

Seeds are carefully cleaned and sorted — only the finest, fully formed seeds make the cut.

03
Cold Press

Traditional Kachi Ghani stone pressing below 40°C — slow rotation that never uses heat, preserving everything.

04
Bottle & Seal

Filled into dark glass bottles with a QR-coded batch label — sealed same day for maximum freshness.

Our Heritage

Punjab in Every Drop

The word "Salaria" carries the weight of history — a name that echoes through villages of Punjab, tied to honest work, fertile land, and a culture that has always believed in sharing the best of what it grows.

We are not just selling oil. We are preserving a way of life — the village chakki, the scent of freshly pressed mustard filling the early morning air, the knowledge passed from grandmother to granddaughter about which oil makes rotis taste like home.

That knowledge, that heritage, that flavour — we are bottling it carefully for a generation that deserves to know where their food truly comes from.
