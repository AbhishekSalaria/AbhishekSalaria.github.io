Our Story

Rooted in the Soil of Punjab

Salaria Naturals was born from a simple belief — that the most nourishing foods come straight from the earth, untouched by chemicals and uncompromised by shortcuts.

Founded in the heartland of India's most fertile agricultural state, we work directly with generations-old farming families. Every bottle of our oil carries the warmth of Punjab's sun and the wisdom of farmers who have worked this land for centuries.

We use the ancient Kachi Ghani cold-press method — slow, patient, preserving every nutrient and the full-bodied flavour that only real mustard oil possesses.

Our Heritage

Punjab in Every Drop

The word "Salaria" carries the weight of history — a name that echoes through the villages of Punjab, tied to honest work, fertile land, and the generosity of a culture that has always believed in sharing the best of what it grows.

We are not just selling oil. We are preserving a way of life — the village chakki, the scent of freshly pressed mustard filling the early morning air, the knowledge passed from grandmother to granddaughter about which oil makes rotis taste like home.

That knowledge, that heritage, that flavour — we're bottling it, carefully, for a generation that deserves to know where their food truly comes from.
